SeMI Network Nodes

Weaviate not only allows you to build custom knowledge networks, you can also tap into exsisting knowledge networks (like plug-in libraries) with nodes containing data that is commonly used. The networking product Keyper allows you to manage your networks and nodes.

Currently-available knowledge networks include:

Common Knowledge Network-node

Fed by the largest encyclopedia in the world, Wikipedia, the Common Knowledge Network gives insights about an enormous range of subjects.

Small and medium-sized Enterprises Network-node

The SME network contains an ever-growing knowledge graph of public SME data. Ideal for enhancing your own data sets.

Weaviate Keyper

Govern your custom networks with Keyper

Keyper is a simple tool that helps you to create and manage networks. You can create keys for your peers and with Weaviate Whitelabel Platform you can monetize the network queries if desired.
