Services

SeMI enables you to build knowledge graphs from the middle of your technology stack all the way to the end-users. We help through software products and consultancy services to create the best outcome possible.

Weaviate at the core

At the core of all our solutions lies the knowledge graph Weaviate which exposes all data through their API. Weaviate can run in the public cloud, private cloud or on-prem and allows you to build a multitude of use cases on top.
